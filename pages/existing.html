Existing Product Activation

Guidelines for submitting proposals

What to Include

  • Activation Mechanics: Outline the activation process
  • Support Activities: Sampling, promotions, giveaways
  • Activation Dates: Tentative dates
  • Branch Selection: List of selected branches
  • Brand Ambassadors: Uniform images, BA details, ID numbers, food handling certificates (if food), ESG compliance
  • Coverage & Media: Model release forms for media or influencer coverage
  • Social Media: Recent high-resolution product pack shots
